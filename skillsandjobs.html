Skills and Jobs:

JOB DESCRIPTION #1 – Software Engineer

JOB TITLE:  Software Engineer

JOB TYPE: Temporary Full-Time (6 month contract)

REPORTS TO: Programming Lead

MAIN DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES:

- Coding and debugging the application

- Other tasks as directed by Programming Lead

QUALIFICATIONS:

-  Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology or similar

 - 2 Years relevant work experience in the field

- Experience and a high knowledge of Java

SKILLS:

- Vast knowledge of computer programming and Java coding.

- Excellent verbal and written communication skills

- Organised and able to meet deadlines

- Solution driven

- Attention to detail, and the ability to make comments in your code

JOB DESCRIPTION #2 – Graphic Designer 

JOB TITLE:  Graphic Designer

JOB TYPE: Temporary Full-Time (6 month contract)

REPORTS TO: Programming Lead

MAIN DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES:

- Designing artwork and graphics for application

- Designing interface for application

- Other tasks as directed Programming Lead

QUALIFICATIONS:

- Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design, User Experience or similar

- 2 Years relevant work experience in the field

- Experience and a high knowledge of graphic editing applications (Photoshop, Lightroom)

SKILLS:

- Excellent verbal and written communication

- Organised and able to meet deadlines

- Attention to detail and accuracy

- Exceptional creativity

JOB DESCRIPTION #3 – Programming Lead 

JOB TITLE:  Programming Lead

JOB TYPE: Temporary Full-Time (6 month contract)

 REPORTS TO: Programming Lead

MAIN DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES:

- Drive time sensitive results

- Being a mentor / tutor for your staff – sharing your knowledge with them

- Making quick decisions to determine the best outcome

QUALIFICATIONS:

- Bachelor’s degree in Management, Programming or similar related field.

- 4 years relevant work experience in the field

- Experience and a high knowledge of Java

- Experience with leadership of a team

SKILLS:

- Able to successfully reach targets and deadlines.

- Excellent verbal and written communication

- Well organised

- Able to drive results

- A team player

- Attention to detail

- Intricate knowledge of Java, and the ability to share that knowledge with your team

 

JOB DESCRIPTION #4 – Technical Support 

JOB TITLE:  Technical Support

JOB TYPE: Temporary Full-Time (6 month contract)

REPORTS TO: Programming Lead

MAIN DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES:

- Troubleshooting system errors

- Providing technical support if needed

- Maintenance and upkeep of server, and firewalls.

- Other tasks as directed by Programming Lead

QUALIFICATIONS:

- Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Computer Science or IT Technician, or related.

- Relevant work experience in the field

SKILLS:

- Excellent verbal and written communication

- Organised and able to troubleshoot various IT related problems

- Advanced knowledge of operating systems

- Analytical and problem solving skills

- Attention to detail
